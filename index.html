Taco Trivia

Question 1: What is the traditional Mexican dish consisting of folded or rolled tortillas filled with various ingredients such as meat, beans, and cheese?

Answer: Tacos.


Question 2: When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Answer: Jeffrey M. Pilcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in a newspaper from 1905. (Source: Smithsonian Magazine )


Question 3: In Mexico, what is the term for a street vendor who sells tacos?

Answer: Taquero or Taquera.


Question 4: What is the name of the spicy sauce or condiment commonly served with tacos, made from chili peppers and other ingredients?

Answer: Salsa.


Question 5: What is the name of the Mexican dish that features small, bite-sized tacos often served as appetizers?

Answer: Tacos de Bistec or Taquito
